Our product

Flux is a solution for a problem that a lot of employees experience, the inability to say no. This results in getting overloaded with work which causes more problems. This is why we developed a product that helps with reducing disturbances and distractions. So with our product people can keep a higher workflow with less stress, so they feel more comfortable and get better results.

Measurements

By capturing different data about the user, Flux can calculate a person’s stress level. The flux wristband is lightweight, small and comfortable to wear. The user won’t even notice it. The flux wristband measures the following data:

  • Heartbeat
  • Amount of tasks
  • Noise level
  • Body temperature

Using Flux

Flux calculates the user’s stress level by measuring different data. When it signals that the user is getting stressed it sends a signal to the light system which changes color. The light system is strategically placed just outside of the user’s office. This is how it shows other employees whether or not they can come inside and disturb the user.

If the light is orange, they can only come in when it’s important or urgent. When the light is red, they should respect the person’s privacy and come back later. This is how Flux helps employees who normally can’t say no to others by helping them not getting disturbed at all.
